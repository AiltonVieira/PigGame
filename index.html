Pig Game

Tutorial

  • Click New Game and set the goal, then click Start.
  • Click Roll Dice to score.
  • Click Hold to get the current score or try to scroll to accumulate score.
  • Clicking the Hold button will add to your score the value of the round, and will change the active player.
  • Win the game who gets the set score before the start.

Dice Tricks

  • One-sided die 1: Zero the round score and change the active player.
  • Two dice with face 6: Reset the active player's score and change the active player.
